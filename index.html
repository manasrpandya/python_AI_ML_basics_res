Hackathon Uno 2024

Welcome to Hackathon Uno! This is a challenge designed for students from ITTMZ to learn and explore AI/ML concepts from the ground up. This hackathon is divided into two:

1) The learning (Present - 20th Nov) :- This hackathon is worked out in the assumption that the participant is a complete beginner. Having said that, before the actual commencement of the hackathon, the participant is advised to go through a few weeks of somewhat intensive learning, wherein the participant is expected to dedicate some time daily to effective learning of the basics required to participate in the hackathon. The directions that the participants may want to follow (instead of searching everything by themselves) are given in the below pages.

2)The hacking (20th Nov - 1st Dec) :- Let the hacking begin! (more instructions will be updated soon)
